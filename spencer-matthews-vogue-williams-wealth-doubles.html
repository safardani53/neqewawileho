Spencer Matthews and Vogue Williams' wealth DOUBLES to 47million in a year

Publish date: 2024-04-16

REALITY TV star Spencer ­Matthews and wife Vogue ­Williams’ wealth has more than doubled in a year — from £21million to £47million.

Spencer, 33, who shot to fame on Made In Chelsea, has hit No2 in our Fabulous mag reality rich list as his high-end property investments surge in value to £15million.

And he is also raking in the cash as the CEO of CleanCo Ventures Ltd, which became one of the UK’s most successful start-ups when it grew by 700 per cent in last year’s lockdown. His shares are now worth £28million.

Another company, Spencer Matthews Ltd, posted assets of £40,000 for TV work and a range of other investments are valued at £3million.

His Eden Rocks diamond business is dormant but his range of watches is valued at £1million.

Spencer worked as a city trader before finding fame at £50 a day on E4’s series Made In Chelsea in 2011.

Since then, he has appeared on I’m A Celebrity, Celebrity MasterChef and The Jump! where he met his wife, Irish model Vogue, 36.
