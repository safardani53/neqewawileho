Photos Of Kylie Jenner &amp; More Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Whether it’s a lavish Aspen getaway, a Kardashian-Jenner vacation to Lake Tahoe, or a cozy trip to Utah — stars like Kylie Jenner, Nina Dobrev, Rebel Wilson and Lori Harvey love to hit the slopes at the end of the year! Who knew that Kendall Jenner and even Kylie’s 2-year-old daughter Stormi Webster are skilled snowboarders? Even Ciara and her kids, Future Jr., 5, and Sienna, 2, are seasoned pros on the ski slopes! The singer’s other son, baby Win (6 mos.), also made a cameo in photos from the Wilson’s joint ski trip with the Bryant family.

For most celebs, winter vacations are quite common. Why, you ask? The latter stars and many famous faces primarily reside on the west coast for work — therefore, a snowy trip with cooler temperatures is a treat for stars (especially the ones who own a lavish winter wardrobe). Not to mention, it’s a chance for Hollywood’s hottest stars to take the best photos and selfies in their Moncler coats, Fendi boots and other designer threads! — See your favorite stars rocking gorgeous coats on winter vacations:

Kylie Jenner 

It seems as though Kylie Jenner has been on vacation for months. The makeup mogul kicked off the holiday season by heading to the mountains in Aspen, Colorado on December 4, 2020. In this photo (seen above), Kylie was photographed shopping at an outlet with her on-off boyfriend Travis Scott on New Year’s Day. The pair, along with their 2-year-old daughter, rented a multi-million dollar mansion in Aspen with Kris Jenner and boyfriend Corey Gamble and her sister Kendall. The family was seen hitting the slopes in Aspen well into the New Year.

Bella Hadid

It appears as though Bella Hadid joined her good friends, Kendall and Kylie in Aspen. On January 3, the supermodel, who’s recently been working jobs in New York City, was spotted on the ski slopes. She sported a cropped, black puffer jacket over her winter white outfit that read “Wassup” on her top.

Kendall Jenner 

Kendall has been enjoying “sister time” with Kylie shredding the gnar in Aspen. Throughout the trip, the supermodel has been sharing videos of herself snowboarding, riding snow mobiles and playing in the snow. Her Aspen wardrobe has included white and black ski outfits, purple snow goggles and a bright yellow puffer jacket.

Kim Kardashian

The Skims founder fully embraced “FREEZN SZN” in this December 22, 2020, from a family trip to Lake Tahoe. Kim threw up her signature double peace signs while wearing a burgundy puffer jacket and tiger-print pants. The KKW Beauty founder, whose kids were also present, sported a silver metallic snow suit and jacket in a separate video from a ski outing.

The Kardashian-Jenner Family

Kim also shared this scenic snap from the family’s trip to Lake Tahoe, which included Kris, Corey, Kendall, Khloe Kardashian, and exes Scott Disick and Kourtney Kardashian. At the time, Kim was accused of photoshopping Kourtney into the Tahoe snap, after eagle-eyed fans claimed Kourt looked like a “hologram.”

Stormi Webster 

Is there anything Stormi Webster can’t do? The little fashionista — who’s already proven to be a trendsetter with her illustrious sneaker and purse collections — showed off her snowboarding skills in Aspen. Kylie shared a clip of her daughter boarding down a slope, captioned, “My little pro!!!!”

Julianne Hough

Julianne Hough hit the mountains for a snow-filled family trip after Christmas. She showed off a number of different winter looks in multiple Instagram posts. The Dancing With the Stars judge went snowboarding in a yellow puffer jacket and she frolicked in the snow in a bright Kith snowsuit and fur hat. Other snaps showed Julianne in a red puffer jacket, a leather coat with fur and a black jacket with orange interior.

Nina Dobrev

Nina Dobrev is spinning into the New Year on an ice rink in Montana! The Vampire Diaries alum, like her BFF, shared numerous photos and videos from her winter vacation with boyfriend Shawn White. Ironically, the couple appears to be on holiday with Julianne, who shared a snowy Instagram video with Nina. Meanwhile, Nina has shared photos of herself dog-sledding in the snow. The actress, who sported SOREL Tivoli IV boots, enjoyed horse-back riding in a plaid coat and yellow tinted sunglasses.

Rebel Wilson

Rebel Wilson enjoyed a ski vacation with her Pitch Perfect co-star Kelley Jakle over the Christmas holiday. The comedian shared a number of photos in Aspen, Colorado that showed her wearing a black Fendi ski suit and Salomon boots. “Fair to say we CRUSHED skiing,” Rebel wrote alongside a solo snap of herself on December 19, 2020. Her boyfriend, Jacob Busch, was also present during the snow-filled vacation.

Ciara & Her Kids

Ciara is another star who wants her kids to hit the slopes at a young age. The “Beauty Marks” singer taught her oldest son Future and her daughter Sienna how to ski this winter. She also shared a ton of cute snaps during a holiday ski vacation with BFF Vanessa Bryant and her kids.
