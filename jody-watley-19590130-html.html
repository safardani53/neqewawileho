Jody Vanessa Watley is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, and artist whose music crosses genres such as pop, R&B, jazz, dance, and Electronic soul. In the late 1970s and early 1980s, she was a member of the r & b / funk group Shalamar, having recorded many hits, especially in the UK. In 1987, she won the Grammy Award for Best New Artist and was nominated for three Grammy Awards. In 2008, she was the recipient of the Billboard Lifetime Achievement Award and also appeared prominently in the 2008 historical black issue of Vogue Italia. In December 2016, Billboard named her as 21st successful dance artist of all time. . and in 2017, the Black Music Honors TV special recognized Watley as a crossover music icon for his breakthrough achievements and influence. In August 2018, Billboard ranked Jody Watley as one of the best female artists of all time, at 53rd.
