Nick Cannon ex-girlfriends: Who has the host dated?

Publish date: 2024-10-07

NICK Cannon is the father of seven children with four different baby mamas.

Cannon, 40, recently made headlines after his new talk show premiered on September 27, 2021, which features his kids singing the theme song.

Who has Nick Cannon dated?

Over the years, Cannon has been linked to many women but has only been married once.

Cannon's ex's include:

Abby De La Rosa

Abby De La Rosa, 30, is a broadcast professional and an international DJ, according to her Instagram bio.

She also has her own business called Masked by La Rose.

In July of 2021, the former pair welcomed twins Zion and Zillion.

Cannon and De La Rosa had previously gotten pregnant in April of 2020 but she suffered a miscarriage.

more from nick cannon

Mariah Carey

Perhaps Cannon's most famous ex is singer Mariah Carey, who is also his former wife.

The tv host and award-winning singer share 10-year-old twins Moroccan and Monroe together.

Out of Cannon's many relationships, Carey is the only one he was actually married to.

Brittany Bell

Cannon and Brittney Bell have two children together Golden, 4, and Powerful Queen, who is 9 months old.

It's not clear exactly when Bell and Cannon ended things, but they began dating sometime in 2015 and have been on-again off-again ever since.

Selita Ebanks

Although Cannon has only been married to Carey, he was previously engaged to model Selita Ebanks.

The couple got together in early 2007 and were engaged in May 2007. However, they separated in October 2007.

It seems to have ended amicably, however, as a spokesperson for Ebanks told People in 2007 that they "still love each other very much and remain the best of friends."

Kim Kardashian

That's right, Cannon and Kim Kardashian briefly dated.

They were together from September 2006 to January 2007, a few years before Kardashian and Kanye West first got together.

Christina Milian

Actress and singer Christina Milian was in the movie Love Don't Cost a Thing with Cannon in 2003. They clearly hit it off, because the two dated from 2003 to 2005.

Milian later claimed Cannon cheated on her, but he said he didn't think they were ever exclusive anyway.

"You got to be in a relationship to cheat," he told radio show Power 106 in September 2019.

Lanisha Cole

Cannon and model Lanisha Cole dated from June to December 2017. Clearly, the man seems to have a type: pretty women, and generally short relationships.

Nicole Scherzinger

Cannon and his former Masked Singer co-star, Nicole Scherzinger, reportedly dated from 1999 to 2000.

Not much is known about their previous relationship.

Jessica White

Model Jessica White and Cannon were first linked in 2015, in the midst of his divorce from Carey.

Their relationship timeline is not totally clear, since White didn't confirm their breakup until August 2020 — after Cannon already had one child with Bell and was expecting again.

“Someone who values you, wouldn’t ever put themselves in a position to lose you,” Jessica wrote on Instagram when she confirmed the pair were no longer together. “I’m so blessed, I’m whole, and ready to embrace a single life free from all baggage, I wish nothing but the best for @nickcannon."

Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas

Cannon and TLC star Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas dated in 2016.

Although the two never seemed to be officially linked, Cannon and Thomas were seen cuddling up together a few times.

Cannon once said of the singer, "I would be so lucky to be able to be called ‘dating’ her. This is all new to me. I’m not ready for titles and all that stuff."

He added that he was taking things slow after his breakup with Carey, but that when it came to Thomas, "She’s somebody I care for and enjoy spending time with."

Alyssa Scott

While there is not much history between Cannon and Alyssa Scott, she is his fourth baby momma.

In July of 2021, Cannon and Scott welcomed baby Zen.

Scott is known as a social media influencer and model and has over 160,000 followers.

Back in October of 2020, they were spotted out on a date in celebration of her birthday.

In a special 2021 Father Day post, Scott shared an Instagram story picture that showed her leaning on a boulder with Cannon grabbing her belly.

Scott captioned the post: "Celebrating you today," with a red heart emoji.

What has Nick Cannon said about future children?

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, Cannon opened up about the possibility of having more children in the future and revealed his therapist recommended waiting a while.

“My therapist says I should be celibate,” he told the media outlet. “I’m not sitting out here planning it.”

He later went on to reveal that none of his children were "accidents."

“Trust me, there’s a lot of people that I could’ve gotten pregnant that I didn’t,” he continued. “The ones that got pregnant are the ones that were supposed to get pregnant."

And while he might be taking a break from having children, Cannon went on to say that he might not stop at seven.
