Bill Rancic and Giuliana DePandi Get Married

Publish date: 2024-03-10

You’re married!

Bill Rancic, The first Apprentice winner, heard those lovely words this weekend, when he wed E! News anchor Giuliana DePandi on Saturday on the Italian island of Capri.

According to People magazine, the couple exchanged vows at the end of a church aisle strewn with white rose petals and dotted with white candles.

“This is the real deal,” said Rancic, who has attended church every Sunday with DePandi since the two became serious, “so we wanted a very old school, full Catholic mass.”

After the nuptials, the pair arranged for buses to take the 190 guests to Capri’s center piazza, where dozens of Neapolitan dancers sang and danced alongside the guests as they walked in a group, led by the bride and groom, to the reception at the Grand Hotel Quisisana.

This marked the second big celebrity wedding in memory, although it received far less hate mail than that of Usher and Tameka Foster.

One prominent person from Ranci’s past was not present, however: his former boss, Donald Trump.

“His schedule didn’t allow it,” said the groom.
