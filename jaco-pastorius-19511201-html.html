John Francis Anthony "Jaco" Pastorius III is an American jazz bassist who was a member of the Weather Report from 1976 to 1981. He worked with Pat Metheny and Joni Mitchell, and recorded albums as well. is the solo artist and conductor of the group. His bass play uses funk, lyrical solo, and creative bass chord. In 2017, he was the only electric bassist out of seven to be introduced to the Jazz DownBeat Jazz Hall of Fame, and was hailed as one of the best electric bass players of all time. Pastorius suffered from addiction and mental health problems throughout his working life, and although widely recognized, in the later stages of his life he still had difficulty keeping a job. due to lack of reliability. Often financially troubled, he was often homeless in the mid-1980s. He died in 1987 from wounds during a battle outside a South Florida club. After his death, his work continued to affect musicians.
