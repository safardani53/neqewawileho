Is Fender better than Gibson?

Both guitars can be used in similar settings in blues and rock; however, Fenders are used more in modern music, country, funk, and overall where a lower gain brighter tone is needed, whereas Gibsons tend to be more friendly to high gain and are used more in hard rock.

Is PRS or Fender better?

Generally, the PRS Custom 24 is better for metal and hard rock than the Stratocaster. This is because the Strats single coil pickups can produce a humming sound when you crank the gain up. But the PRS uses humbuckers which allows you to produce a warm, beefy tone, without suffering from any humming issues.
