Joanna Krupa DWTS Mambo Marathon (VIDEO)

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Joanna Krupa and Derek Hough won the night on the hit ABC reality show Dancing With The Stars on Monday. Their waltz earned them 26 points and their mambo marathon victory gave them the top score of the evening with a combined 36 points.

Hovering at the bottom the the leaderboard are Michael Ervin, Louis Vito, and Melissa Joan Hart. With two contestants going home tonight, it should be interesting to see what happens.

The first dancer to be eliminated will be based upon viewers votes that were cast last night. The remaining bottom two pairs will be pitted against each other in a dance-off that will be judged by Carrie Ann Inaba, Len Goodman and Bruno Tonioli.
