MISSISSIPPI rapper Rick Ross has enjoyed a long career in hip hop with multiple hits under his belt.

But here is what we know about his current dating life.

Who is Rick Ross dating?

Rick Ross, whose real name is William Leonard Roberts II, is more famous for his music than his dating life.

However, he is currently rumored to be dating a Tanzanian social media influencer named Hamisa Mobetto.

Rick Ross has not confirmed this rumor, but has only given the media a hint as to what is going on with his love life.
