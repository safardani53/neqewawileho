Im 43 but people say I look half my age its all thanks to my 3 secret I have daily

Publish date: 2024-08-26

A 43-YEAR-OLD woman, who has been described as looking half her age, has revealed one of the secrets to her youthful appearance. 

Amy-Jo Simpson looks incredible and has been showered with compliments about her complexion and figure. 

So how does she manage to keep ageing at bay and remain fighting fit? 

The influencer, who can be found at her TikTok profile @amyjosimpson, has previously shared that she swears by one particular morning ritual – a juice that she claims works wonders. 

In a video posted to her social media profile, she lifted the lid on her health hack, and how you can make it at home too.

Holding up a green smoothie, she explained: “This is the kind of juice I drink every single day, and I have been for years and years.

“This one is called nature explosion.”

The juice’s ingredients can be bought for an approximate total of £3.30 from budget supermarkets like Aldi and Lidl. 

They include a handful of spinach, half a celery stick, two green apples, a quarter of a courgette and a lime.

The juice’s recipe also contains half an avocado, a quarter of a cucumber, one carrot, 2cm chunk of broccoli stem and a pint of iced water.

Amy-Jo – who has been open about going through the perimenopause – then shared the step-by-step recipe. 

She continued to her 2.5 million followers: “If you struggle to get your greens in, this is such a good way of getting that into your body.

“Because it’s a juice, it’s done very quickly – even if you don’t like it.

“I’m a bit of a weirdo, I like veggie-based juices, so in this one you’ve only got the apple and the lime to take away that kind of veggie taste – but I like it!”

She added: “It is strong, I’m not going to lie, but it is so good. I always feel so detoxed, even after one juice.

“All of my juices are brilliant for your skin, your hair, your mood.” 

She then said that this juice in particular also helps to give her “so much energy”, alongside a daily shot of turmeric and ginger – and a good cup of tea.

I always feel so detoxed, even after one juice

After she had chopped all of her ingredients, Amy-Jo added some cold water until it was filled up to the max line on her blender.

She then blitzed all the ingredients, before leaving it to settle, and then pressed the start button again until fully blended and ready to serve with ice.. 

And her daily £3 juice certainly seems to be doing the trick in turning back the hands of time, as many people took the time to comment on her video. 

One wrote: “You look bloody amazing and half my age and we're the same age.”

A second said: “Stunning!” As a third added: “Awesome!”
