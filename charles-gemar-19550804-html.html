Charles Donald "Sam" Gemar is a former American NASA astronaut and lieutenant colonel in the United States Army. Gemar flew three space shuttle missions. Gemar completed 385 Earth orbits and over 581 hours in space. He also held various positions at NASA, including CAPCOM for space shuttle missions. Gemar was the first astronaut to be born in South Dakota.
