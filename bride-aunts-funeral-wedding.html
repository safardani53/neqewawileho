Bride combines aunt's open coffin funeral with her WEDDING to save on church costs

Publish date: 2024-06-19

A FRUGAL bride has combined her aunt’s open casket funeral with her own wedding to save on church costs.

The woman told her Facebook friends that the dead body of her elderly relative would be “on display” while she exchanged vows with her partner.

She said the bizarre decision was prompted by funeral costs “skyrocketing.”

The astonishing post was shared on social media account That's It I'm Wedding Shaming where "horrified" users slammed the bride’s "weird" plans.

In the original post, the unnamed woman wrote: “Some of you may have gotten the news already - but Aunt Karen has sadly passed away from natural causes.

"We are all deeply saddened by her loss but we promise we will not cancel the wedding next week.

“In her honour, we know she would want us to celebrate and have a day filled with love and joy.

"I would also like to add that funeral costs are skyrocketing and we will be having an open casket session during some of the wedding processions.”

We will be having an open casket session during some of the wedding processions

The bride then urged friends of her Aunt Karen “to stop by” and view her corpse during her nuptials.

She said: “Feel free to stop by during the wedding to say goodbyes, she will be on display for those who wish to gain some closure.

“Remember, even though losing ones we love can be heartbreaking - there is also a joy in celebrating love and partnerships in marriage.

"We are excited to see you all there on Saturday".

Her post has caused outrage among Facebook users.

One wrote: “She will be on display' is the icing on this weird, weird cake of an event."

Another posted: "So it's a joint wedding and funeral to save on church costs? So bizarre".

A third wrote: "I'm all for honouring the woman, but a joint wedding/funeral is weird"
