Prince Harry hangs at a bar with Rob McElhenney and TV cast without Meghan Markle in new pic

Publish date: 2024-02-18

A NEW photo of Prince Harry hanging out with a famous TV cast without Meghan Markle is circulating on Instagram.

Actor Rob McElhenney, who stars in It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia, posted a selfie on Monday that showed him posing with his co-star Glenn Howerton and the Duke of Sussex.

In the shot, Prince Harry, 39, beamed wearing a black shirt and matching blazer, while standing next to Rob, 46, and Glenn, 47 in a bar.

The royal appeared to be flying solo, as there was no sign of wife Meghan.

The photo of Prince Harry was snapped back in September when the trio attended a football match between Inter Miami CF and Los Angeles at the FC BMO Stadium in LA.

Harry joined an A-list crowd - which included Leonardo DiCaprio, Selena Gomez, Owen Wilson and Jason Sudeikis - as Lionel Messi lead his team to a 3-1 win against Los Angeles.

Messi's Inter Miami team is co-owned by David Beckham, who is rumoured to have fallen out with Prince Harry, and the duke was seen walking straight past Brooklyn Beckham and wife Nicola Peltz.

Rob’s Instagram caption read: “Aside from that slight allergic reaction to the nuts, 2023 was one of the best years of my life.

“Thank you to the people and places that made it possible.

“My life is full of love and joy because of you.”

“So excited for 2024. I’ll stay away from the nuts.”

Other photos in his New Year post included co-star Charlie Day and Rob’s wife Kaitlin Olson.

There was also a snap with Ryan Reynolds, who co-owns the Wrexham football team with him.

Prince Harry wasn’t the only royal to feature in Rob's end-of-year post.

There was a photo with King Charles and Queen Camilla, who starred in a season two episode of the TV documentary Welcome to Wrexham, which revolves around Rob and Ryan’s football club.

In a trailer for the latest season, Deadpool actor Ryan says: “So, the King of England called.

“We went to monarchy boot camp.

“It’s like the military except your pinky’s always up.”

Showing the owners learning how to walk, shake hands and sit in front of royalty, the show’s Instagram page said: “Rob and Ryan get a crash course in royal etiquette.”

Prince Harry has a number of famous faces in his inner circle.

Sixteen-time Grammy-winning songwriter David Foster and his fifth wife Katharine McPhee are said to be part of their inner circle.

American Idol runner up Katharine said: “My husband has a beautiful relationship with Harry, like father and son.”

They are also said to be “very good friends” with Oprah Winfrey, who they granted their first interview to after quitting as members of the Royal Family.

Harry has a connection with Saturday Night Fever star John Travolta as the actor danced with Princess Diana at the White House in 1985.

Meghan and her husband reportedly got on well with the star when they met at The Beverly Hills Hotel in Los Angeles.

They hung out in the VIP Polo Lounge, but it is unclear whether this will be a long-term association.
