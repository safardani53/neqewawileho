Paul Lorin Kantner is an American rock musician. He is best known as the co-founder, rhythm guitarist and occasional singer of Jefferson Airplane, a leading psychedelic rock band from the anti-cultural era. He continued in these roles as a member of Jefferson Starship, the successor group to Jefferson Airplane. Jefferson Airplane was founded in 1965 when Kantner met Marty Balin. Kantner eventually became the band's leader and led the band through a period of great success in the late 1960s. In 1970, while still active with Jefferson Airplane, Kantner and several Bay Area musicians took over. recorded a unique side project, Blows Against the Empire under the name "Paul Kantner / Jefferson Starship". Jefferson Airplane continued to record and perform until 1973. Kantner renamed Jefferson Starship in 1974 and continued to record and perform with them until 1984. He then led a reformed Jefferson Starship from 1992 until his death in 2016 .
