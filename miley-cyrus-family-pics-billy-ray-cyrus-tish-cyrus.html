Billy Ray Cyrus, Tish &amp; The Gang Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-29

You all might already be rather familiar with the Cyrus family. Miley Cyrus is perhaps the most popular member of her family, as she has a whopping 107 million followers.

Though, while her dad and siblings may have far less followers than her, that doesn’t mean they haven’t built quite the fan base themselves through their own musical careers. Billy Ray Cyrus is an American singer-songwriter and actor who has released 16 studio albums and 53 singles since 1992,    

32-year-old Brandi Cyrus, 31-year-old Trace Cyrus, and 20-year-old Noah Cyrus have all obviously followed in their father’s footsteps as well. In 2009, Brandi collaborated with celebrity friends to form the band Frank & Derol; in 2006, Trace signed a recording contract with Columbia Records with his pop rock band Metro Station; and in 2016, Noah dropped her first ever single “Make Me (Cry).”
