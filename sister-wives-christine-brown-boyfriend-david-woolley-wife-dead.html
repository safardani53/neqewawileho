Sister Wives star Christine Browns boyfriend David Woolleys wife tragically died 10 years befo

Publish date: 2024-07-21

SISTER Wives star Christine Brown’s new boyfriend David Woolley’s wife tragically died 10 years before he met the reality star, The U.S. Sun has learned.

Christine, 50, and David, 59, went public with their relationship on Valentine's Day, but had been secretly dating since late last year.

The U.S. Sun has learned that David became a widow to his eight children after his late wife, Margaret Lucille Suliin Woolley, tragically passed away.

Her cause of death is not currently public, but according to an obituary found online, she died on June 20, 2012 in her native Utah.

She was born in San Diego - just like David - and passed away at 43 years old.

Margaret and David had gotten married in November 1990, and were together for 22 years up until her death.

A LOVING MOTHER OF EIGHT

According to her sister Debbie Suliin-Drew, Margaret was very close to her children and loved them very much.

"The only thing I will say is that my sister loved her kids more than anything," Debbie, who owns a candy shop close by to David, told The U.S. Sun in an exclusive statement.

Margaret is survived by her children- Adam, Colton, Garrett, Kati, Dallas, Raegan, Payton and Maddox, whom she shared with David.

David's late wife's obituary read: "Margaret was very charitable, loving, and caring to family and friends.

"She had compassion to help others. She loved her children unconditionally.

"We loved our mom for who she was and what she did. We will miss her adventurous spirit."

Her children still honor their mother until this day, and continue to post childhood photos with her online.

LOVE AFTER LOSS

David, who owns a construction company, is very much present in his children and grandchildren's lives.

In social media posts, he has shared pictures with his grandkids and wrote, "I love being a grandpa" and "grandkids are the best." He even brought them to Disneyland.

One daughter, Kati Charlene, has been supportive of her father's new girlfriend.

She posted TikTok videos and told her followers that he "loves Christine" and said the Sister Wives star is "nicer" in person.

Christine, for her part, has six children and three grandchildren - her daughter Mykelti is to Avalon, one, and newborn twins Archer Banks and Ace McCord.

Between David and Christine, the new couple have been spending a lot of time with their kids and grandkids.

On February 14, Christine made her relationship Instagram official by sharing pics with the David Woolley Drywall founder.

"I finally found the love of my life, David. The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took it’s first breath.

"He’s wonderful and kind, incredible with my children and an adorable grandpa.

"I never dreamed I could find a love like this," she wrote.

A few days later, David professed his love to his "soulmate" by sharing with his followers a sweet message.

"When I’m with you, we just sit in our own little bubble and the world can go around us and we’re just standing still and it’s perfect and I have never felt like this before.

"When I took you to the The Little Mermaid play I saw how much you loved life and I knew I had some one special for me. I’m the luckiest guy, thank you for finding me. Love, your king," he captioned a flattering photo of the mom of six.

MOVING ON TOGETHER

The happy couple aren't hiding their love for each other.

In exclusive photos obtained by The U.S. Sun, David and Christine celebrated Valentine's Day at a speakeasy close by to her home in Murray, Utah.

They packed on the PDA by "holding hands," "constantly laughing," and David even was seen putting his hand on her lower back.

The newfound love comes over a year after Christine announced her split from ex Kody.

The exes announced their split in November 2021.

Other wife Janelle Brown, who was spiritually married to Kody, revealed her split from Kody in December, the same time Meri Brown announced she had separated from the family's patriarch as well.

Kody remains legally married to suspected "favorite" wife Robyn Brown.

Season 18 is currently filming, with a premiere date not announced yet, and Christina has teased her boyfriend David will be making an appearance.
