How many kids does Clay Walker have?

Publish date: 2024-05-13

CLAY Walker is known for his hit singles What's It to You and Live Until I Die.

The country music star has several children from his past marriage to Lori Lampson and from his second marriage to Jessica Craig.

How many kids does Clay Walker have?

Clay Walker has seven children: two older daughters from ex-wife Lori Lampson and five more kids from second wife Jessica Craig.

The Texas-born singer's kids include:

  • MaClay DaLayne (January 14, 1996)
  • Skylor ClayAnne (May 14, 1999)
  • William Clayton (August 5, 2008)
  • Mary Elizabeth (December 27, 2009)
  • Elijah Craig (November 4, 2017)
  • Ezra Stephen (November 4, 2017)
  • Christiaan Michael (January 5, 2021)

On April 11, 2023, Clay revealed he and Jessica are expecting their sixth child together; which means he'll soon have eight kids in total.

When did Clay marry Jessica Craig?

On September 28, 2007, Clay tied the knot for a second time with model Jessica Craig.

The pair held their wedding ceremony at the Audubon Zoo in New Orleans.

"This is one of the happiest days of my life," Clay said at the time to CMT.

"I am married to a beautiful girl, my daughters are with me and I am with friends. What more could a guy ask for?"
