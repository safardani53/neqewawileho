Publish date: 2024-03-10

A former '90s TV star and an ex-boy band member revealed he is expecting a second child with his wife.

Marques Houston, 42, announced his partner, Miya Dickey, 22, is pregnant again. 

In an Instagram collection, the Sisters Sisters alum uploaded a series of black and white snapshots of Miya showing off her baby bump. 

In the series, the makeup artist took a stroll across the beach as the waves came crashing in. 

Miys flaunted her baby belly as she donned a strapless white lace top and matching skirt. 

The Immature/IMx singer captioned the post: "This incredible woman is having another child!

The pair welcomed their first child, daughter Zara Denise, in December 2021.  

SISTER, SISTER

In 1994, Marques appeared on the ABC sitcom as the next-door neighbor, Roger Evans, of twins Tamera Campbell (Tamera Mowry) and Tia Landry (Tamera Mowry).

During the family comedy, the twin sisters frequently rejected the advances of Roger.

Roger was constantly being told: "Go Home, Roger!"

When the show ended in 1999, the TV star focused on his music career with six solo albums. 

THE WEDDING

In August 2020, Marques and Miya married at a small ceremony at Eagle Glen Golf Club in Corona, California.

According to Us Weekly, the guest list amid the coronavirus pandemic included the groom’s Immature/IMx bandmates, Jerome Jones and Kelton Kessee, and recording artist MAJOR.

Miya changed her profile name on social media to Miya Houston earlier in 2022.

After the two confirmed their relationship in October 2018, he proposed to her in March 2019. 

They started dating when she was 18 and walked down the aisle in 2020 when she was 19.

'WE FELL IN LOVE'

In June 2020, Marques took to Instagram and defended himself against rumors that they began dating when she was underage.

In this lengthy statement, the R&B singer said: "I met Miya in 2018 through a mutual friend. Never before then, did I even know she existed."

He continued: "It was then that Chrissy and Miya became friends. 

"Miya and I didn’t start dating until she was an adult. We fell in love and got engaged in March of 2019.
