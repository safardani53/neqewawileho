Chris Klein Expecting Second Child WithWife Laina Rose

Publish date: 2024-03-20

Oh, baby! Chris Klein is expecting his second child with his wife, Laina Rose.

“My beautiful angel @lainarose is making another beautiful angel. Easton’s going to be a big Brother. 24 weeks along. We are so #blessed,” the American Pie alum wrote on Wednesday, January 24, via Twitter.

The pair, who tied the knot in August 2015 after four years of dating, are already parents to 18-month-old son Frederick Easton. They announced their engagement in December 2014. 

Rose also shared the exciting news of their new addition  on social media.

“Excited to share that our little Man will become a Big Brother this May. We are so excited for the adventures ahead and the joy that will fill our 2018.💕,” Rose wrote on Wednesday via Instagram. 

She added in a second post on Thursday, January 25, “Two more from yesterday because he is so stinking cute and I try so hard not to overload my feed with kid photos but sometimes I just can’t help myself.”

The travel agent frequently shares family photos via Instagram.

Klein was previously engaged to actress Katie Holmes. The duo split in March 2005. It was late ‘90s, young Hollywood, and we were having a good time … not unlike all those vampires today.”
