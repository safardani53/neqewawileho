Angelina Pivarnick Shows Off $60,000 Engagement Ring

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Jersey Shore's Angelina Pivarnick tells UsMagazine.com that she was completely "surprised" when boyfriend David Kovacs got down and one knee and proposed to her at the STYLE360 Sachika's Fashion Show Wednesday.

In fact when Us caught up with her at the Indashio Show later that afternoon, Pivarnick, 24, said that the $60,000, 2-karat diamond ring was the best part about getting engaged.

"I don't know if that's the right way to say it, but it's so freaking nice!" said the reality star. "I freaking love it; he did a good job!"

But the platinum sparkler's dazzle hadn't kept Pivarnick and Kovacs, who are from the same neighborhood in Staten Island, from at least thinking about wedding details.

"I want a really big one with hundreds of people and a beautiful show-stopping dress that suits me!" Pivarnick said. "It will have lots of Italian traditions, and more specifically — Hungarian/Italian traditions — food and music. We're both Hungarian/Italian, that's just one more thing we have in common."
