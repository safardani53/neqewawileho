Four people including 12-year-old girl hospitalised after horror car crash in South Lanarkshire

Publish date: 2024-09-25

FOUR people including a 12-year-old girl have been injured in a serious road crash in Scotland.

The accident, which involved a Seat Leon and a Dacia Logan, happened at 8.50am on Sunday on the A71 at Drumclog, Strathaven in South Lanarkshire.

A 46-year-old woman who was driving the Seat was taken to Wishaw General Hospital, where her condition is described as stable.

Her two passengers, a 12-year-old girl and a 50-year-old man, were also taken to the same hospital but they have since been released.

The 59-year-old male driver of the Dacia was taken by ambulance to Crosshouse Hospital, Kilmarnock, and allowed to go home after treatment.

Police Scotland have appealed for witnesses following the accident.

Constable David Foster said: "It is important that we speak to anyone who witnessed the crash to establish the full circumstances of what happened."
