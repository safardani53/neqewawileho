Erin Andrews: Engaged to Jarret Stoll!

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Erin Andrews confirmed her engagement to her longtime boyfriend, NHL star Jarret Stoll, by debuting a massive diamond ring at LAX.

After hinting that this event had taken place by talking about her "ring game" on Twitter, the Fox Sports staple cleared up any doubts.

The Dancing With the Stars co-host, 38, rocked the diamond ring, which sounds like it is not small or cheap, strolling through the airport.

Andrews first hinted at this Thursday, tweeting at Stoll, "This weather at Lambeau is really going to put a damper on my ring game @jarretstoll."

She was referring to the ring … and the home of the Green Bay Packers and some of the coldest weather in the National Football League.

Immediately, fans figured this out and started tweeting back their congratulations, but the sideline reporter stayed quiet about it at the time.

Her former Dancing With the Stars pro partner, Maksim Chmerkovskiy, spilled the beans for her during an interview with Fox, however.

Asked to name his best partners of all time, Maks named Meryl Davis, with whom he finally won the Mirrorball trophy in Season 18.

He also named Erin, who "maybe just got engaged."

Chmerkovskiy jokingly called it "payback" for when she revealed that his fiancée, Peta Murgatroyd, was pregnant on Dancing With the Stars.

Andrews has kept her relationship with Stoll under wraps, especially after he was arrested for possession of cocaine and ecstasy in 2015.

Stoll got popped in Las Vegas for trying to sneak these drugs into a party, which his then-girlfriend seemed to reference on TV afterward.

With Willow Shields having just completed a crazy performance in honor of Spring Break, Erin asked if she ever went wild on vacation.

No, The Hunger Games star replied, "I just sit at home and watch movies all week." There’s no shame in that, Erin Andrews responded.

"I feel like some other people should learn from you."

Some other people like her own significant other?

She didn’t say, but it was pretty obvious at the time. Not that it cost him; Jarret has now put a ring on it and locked down Erin, for life!

Congratulations to the happy couple!
