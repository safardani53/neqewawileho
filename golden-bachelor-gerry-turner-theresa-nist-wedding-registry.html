SUSAN'S RETURN

This week, it was revealed that an eliminated contestant from the reality dating show will officiate Gerry and Theresa's wedding.

On Tuesday, the happy couple's officiant was finally revealed on Instagram.

Susan Noles became a fan favorite throughout her appearance on the show.

Many compared her to Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner, 68, and she even made a special appearance on the show's finale to say hello to Susan.

Susan shared the happy news on her Instagram Feed while posting a picture of her with the iconic couple.

She was wearing an all-black outfit while Theresa wore a floral print shirt and Gerry sported a black jacket with a white button-up shirt.

The post was captioned: "I guess the secret is out! I’m officiating THE GOLDEN WEDDING and I’m just beyond excited!!"

THE ENGAGEMENT

Gerry and Theresa got engaged during the season one finale that aired on November 30, 2023.

According to Cosmopolitan, Turner "presumably proposed" during the week of August 30, 2023.

The couple got engaged in Costa Rica, per Us Weekly.

'HAPPILY EVER AFTER'

As fans anticipate the live wedding, they want to know what to expect.

ABC's description of the program states, in part: "After more than 20 years of fostering love on 'The Bachelor,' 'The Bachelorette' and 'Bachelor in Paradise,' 'The Golden Bachelor' showcases a whole new kind of love story – one for the golden years.

"On this all-new unscripted series, one hopeless romantic is given a second chance at love in the search for a partner with whom to share the sunset years of life.

"After lifetimes of love and loss and a whirlwind journey on 'The Golden Bachelor,' Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist will walk down the aisle to begin their next chapter, happily ever after."
