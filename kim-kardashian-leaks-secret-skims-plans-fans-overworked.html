Kim Kardashian 'blacks out' and accidentally leaks top-secret Skims plans to fans after being 'overw

Publish date: 2024-09-01

KIM Kardashian has been caught on camera as she accidentally leaked top-secret Skims plans to fans.

In the latest episode of The Kardashians, the Skims founder admitted she had a brain freeze while speaking to Today anchor Savannah Guthrie, 51. 

After a seemingly disastrous interview, Kim, 43, walked up to the long line of eager fans in New York and dished her upcoming new men's line for Skims.

In the confessional, the Hulu star admitted: "I looked on TikTok and saw me walking up to people, fans that I know and love, telling them all of Skims secrets - about how we're launching men soon.

"I actually have no recollection of it. I had no idea I stopped at the Tiffany's store and was posting all about it."

An unseen female individual said: "Yeah, you were out of it."

SKIMS FOR MEN

Although Kim revealed the Skims Men's line ahead of its official launch date, word of mouth ended up driving sales.

Near the end of October 26, sources told TMZ that the new fashion line for men had its biggest day of sales.

The Skims website received traffic records and pulled in about 25,000 orders within the first five minutes of its opening.

The shapewear brand expanded into briefs, undershirts, and boxers, as prices for the menswear items range from $16 to $54.

The campaign included shirtless photos of its model lineup including San Francisco 49ers' Nick Bosa, 26, the Brazilian soccer star Neymar, 21, and Oklahoma City Thunders player Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, 25.
