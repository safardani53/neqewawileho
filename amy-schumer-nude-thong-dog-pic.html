Amy Schumer wears nothing but a nude thong in funny pic from one of her 'best moments of the year'

Publish date: 2024-02-19

AMY Schumer poses nearly naked in a nude thong for a hilarious picture - showing her clutching a pup in a hot dog suit.

The US comedian, 36, wowed her fans with the funny and gorgeous kitchen snap on Instagram, as she named it one of the best moments of her year.

The blonde stand-up, who also caught her friend howling with laughter in the background, smiles gamely at the camera in the pic, which features her own dog, called Tati.

The cute pic has attracted almost 70,000 likes and thousands of comments on Instagram, including: "That's how to make a hot dog hot!"

Another fan cooed: "Real woman, no butt shots!" while a third wrote: "Wow you are gorg."

Amy went through a major break-up in May this year when she split from furniture designer boyfriend Ben Hanisch, but she still found ten moments of joy to post about including this pic.

Other proud moments she'd documented in photos included the Women's March at the start of the year which saw her protest against Donald Trump with Madonna.

She also posted a snap of herself performing in the Comedy Cellar in New York with Chris Rock and Dave Chappelle - who she was involved in a pay row with this year.

Fans were outraged when it was revealed she was paid less than Rock and Chappelle by Netflix - though she later said she didn't deserve being paid as much as them as they are the "greatest comics of all time".

In a post under the original hot dog pic in August, Amy wrote: "Thanks for chiming in on what you feel I deserve to be paid. I believe women deserve equal pay.

"However I don't believe I deserve equal pay to Chris and Dave. They are legends and 2 of the greatest comics of all time.

"I would like to say that I have been selling out arenas these last couple years. Something a female comic has never done.

"That's a big deal to me, especially because I know I do my best every night on stage for the audience and they have a good time. I didn't ask for the same as my friends.

"I did ask for more than the initial offer. I will continue to work my ass off and be the best performer I can be. The reports of me 'demanding' or 'insisting' on equal pay to them aren't a true. #fat #stealsjokes," she concluded.
