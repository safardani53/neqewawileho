Vanessa Hudgens and Cole Tuckers Relationship Timeline: Photos

Publish date: 2024-03-26

Vanessa Hudgens found The One in Cole Tucker.

The pair first sparked romance rumors when they were spotted holding hands in November 2020, less than one year after Hudgens split from longtime love Austin Butler. Hudgens and Tucker went Instagram official in honor of Valentine’s Day the following year.

“Me and Cole met on a Zoom meditation group. Very random, yes,” Hudgens told Entertainment Tonight in April 2021. “Zoom, you’ve got to love it. He’s just kind of perfect for me. I am [happy]. I really am.”

One month later, Hudgens elaborated on how she made the first move when she originally met the baseball player. “I fully just slid into his DMs and was like, ‘Hey, it was nice to meet you.’ So I think there is no shame in making the first move,” she said on The Drew Barrymore Show in May 2021.
