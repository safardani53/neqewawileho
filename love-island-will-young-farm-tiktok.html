Where is Love Island star Will Young's farm?

Publish date: 2024-05-30

SWAPPING the quiet countryside for a stylish villa in South Africa is one of this year's Love Island contestants, Will Young.

The hunky farmer, 23, is leaving the grasslands behind for the plains of South Africa in a bid to find romance on the ITV dating show.

Where is Will Young's farm?

Love Island star Will Young's family farm is located in Buckinghamshire, south east England.

The handsome farmer revealed that he has always lived on a farm with his parents - and of course all the sheering sheep.

He said: “I’ve lived at home all my life. I think the longest I’ve been away from home is seven days.

“So I would definitely miss the animals and my family, and I live next door to my grandparents as well. So I think that would be quite tough – getting out of that safe space and chucked in the deep end.

What has Will Young said about his farm?

Before entering the Villa, the 23-year-old revealed why he made the decision to leave the farm life behind and jet off to sunny South Africa.

He explained: "Having grown up on a farm, it’s been quite difficult juggling relationships and work.

''Love Island will give me time away from the farm to solely go out and find love. I think I’m at the time of my life where I’m mature enough to go and find a wife."

He also explained how his decision to look for love on the dating show, has meant his dad has been left short on the farm.

“It took a lot of time setting in place with the farm, obviously, because it’s me and my dad working,” he told us.

“Me getting taken out of it, there was a lot of thought that went into it of different things that we could do. We’ve basically got someone in to replace me while I am away.”
