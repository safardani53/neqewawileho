Publish date: 2024-09-10

A CUTE button nose is the shape that everyone seems to want at present. 

On TikTok, the hashtag “ski slope nose” – the type that inwardly curves into a turned-up tip at the bottom – has racked up almost 25 million views. 

But while some people resort to surgery to get the desirable snout, one influencer claims she can get the look in a couple of seconds with a quick make-up hack that instantly shifts her face shape. 

Briana is a beauty whizz who regularly impresses her 104k followers on her TikTok account @Briclv with her savvy make-up tips. 

And in a recent clip that racked up a huge 5.4 million views, she shared her nifty hack to instantly get the cute button nose look. 

The cosmetics guru began by whipping out an lipliner in a cool brown shade, which you can pick up from Superdrug for less than £3.

She then pushed her nose upwards to create a crinkle just above the tip, in which she drew a line using the liner. 

Briana then repeated the process, drawing another brown line across the top of her septum, attaching her two nostrils. 

Finally, the stunning influencer took a small brush and buffed out the brown lines, until a blended contour effect was left. 

The dark shade gave the effect of a pinched nose, creating the illusion of the sought-after ski slope shape.
