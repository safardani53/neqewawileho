Details On Brothers!

Darez Diggs’s Brother, Stefon Diggs

Stefon Diggs, Darez’s older brother, is a football wide receiver for the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League (NFL). 

Stefon played college football at Maryland and was drafted by the Minnesota Vikings in the fifth round of the 2015 NFL Draft.

Despite his late-round draft status, Stefon became a full-time starter for the Vikings midway through his rookie year. 

Likewise, the player also became part of an effective receiving tandem alongside Adam Thielen, with his highlights including catching the “Minneapolis Miracle” pass during the 2017–18 NFL playoffs.

Stefon received a 5-year extension the following offseason, playing two more seasons in Minnesota before being traded to Buffalo in 2020.

With the Bills, he became the fastest receiver to surpass 100 catches with his new team and broke Eric Moulds’ franchise record for receiving yards in a season.

Moreover, Stefon led all NFL receivers in 2020 with 127 receptions and 1,535 receiving yards.

Darez Diggs Brother, Trevon Diggs

However, he transitioned full-time to cornerback in his sophomore year and had an impressive college career, recording multiple interceptions, tackles, and fumble recoveries.

The Cowboys drafted him in the second round of the 2020 NFL Draft. The player had a breakout season in 2021, leading the NFL with 11 interceptions and earning his first Pro Bowl and All-Pro selections.
