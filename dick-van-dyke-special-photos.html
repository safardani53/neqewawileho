Photos of His 98th Birthday Celebration Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-10-06

Dick Van Dyke celebrated his 98th birthday on December 13, and CBS is honoring the icon with a TV special. See all the best photos of the stars who are celebrating the living legend.

Dick Van Dyke 98 Years of Magic is airing December 21 on CBS and streaming on Paramount+. The TV special is honoring and celebrating the great Dick Van Dyke. Over the course of his decades-long career, Dick has become a Grammy, Emmy, and Tony Award winner.

He remains a beloved Hollywood star after starring in The Dick Van Dyke Show and Mary Poppins. The special will feature performances, special guests, and more. Stars like Julie Andrews, Carol Burnett, Ted Danson, Lin-Manuel Miranda, Rita Ora, and more will be making an appearance on Dick’s special night.
