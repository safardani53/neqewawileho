I tried on my old Halloween costumes from when I was 10 - they are all so tight and most are totally

Publish date: 2024-02-18

FESTIVE days such as Halloween or Christmas often lead you down memory lane.

And Jay couldn't resist trying on her old spooky costumes when she came across a box full of them.

The 22-year-old who goes by CrayCrayJay on social media decided to try each one on - but it didn't go quite to plan.

The Halloween outfits had been sitting in storage at her parents' garage for ten years.

"I thought it would be fun to try these on because I haven't worn these since I was like ten and I know I'm not going to fit in them.

She tried on a mix of Halloween costumes and dance outfits, despite never attending dance class.

The first thing Jay tried on was a neon orange fluffy scarf which can also be turned into a dress thanks to the giant hole in the middle.

"I don't even know if I can get my entire body in here," she claimed after ten years of not wearing it.

While she struggle to pull it up her waist, Jay did manage to get it up although it would have left her shivering on a night out.

The fluffy scarf was totally see-through over her black sports bra.

She also tried on a very interesting dance costume which was a black polka dot dress with an orange corset and a giant stain on the armpit.

After a few jumping manoeuvres, Jay managed to squeeze into the dress adding: "It's so stretched out you can see through it."

Next, Jay tried on a blue and white sequin baseball top and hat which did indeed still fit.

Another Halloween costume in the style of a witch didn't even go up Jay's leg while a blue and black dress left Jay flashing her bum.

An 80s style black, neon pink, and white dress also managed to get on Jay's now-adult body - but it was just a tad short on her legs as it totally left her bum exposed.

A black velvet mini dress looked promising at a size 10-12 but once Jay put it on it wouldn't go over her arms and she was almost stuck inside.

"I don't know if I can get it off," she exclaimed as she stood trapped in the ensemble,

The next outfit was a hippy two-piece set with a blue floral top and tiny pink leggings.

Although Jay couldn't pull the leggings over her bum - she did managed to get the top on and it didn't look too bad considering how much taller she is now.

The clip posted to her YouTube account @craycrayjay was a hit with over 13k views.

People were left in hysterics, one person wrote: "The second costume you put on was HILARIOUS."

Another commented: "Girl I can’t."
