Towies Jake Hall opens up about shock stabbing as he parties at Wireless The Sun

Publish date: 2024-02-21

TOWIE star Jake Hall has said he is lucky to be alive after he was stabbed during a recent break in Marbella.

Speaking exclusively to The Sun Online at Wireless Festival, the 25-year-old said: “I'm feeling great. I'm glad to be alive. The wound is healing well.”

Enjoying the music, he added: “I'm just focusing on work and getting better - nothing else matters.”

Jake was wounded in his right side at the end of May when he was caught up in a fight between two rival gangs while enjoying a night out at the Spanish town’s Aqwa Mist.

The star was rushed to intensive care after the attack, and almost lost a kidney.

He remained abroad for some time while he recovered - during which time he was seen getting a little TLC from his on/off girlfriend Chloe Lewis.
