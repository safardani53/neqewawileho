Tim Gunn Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Bio:
Tim Gunn (born Timothy MacKenzie Gunn in Washington, D.C. on July 29, 1953) is a fashion consultant and television personality. Tim served on faculty at Parsons The New School for Design from 1982 to 2007 and also acted as chair of fashion design at the school from 2000 to 2007. Later he joined Liz Claiborne as Chief Creative Officer.

Best Known For:
Tim is best known for his work on Project Runway alongside Heidi Klum.

Personal Life:
Tim has been very open about the fact that he has not been in a relationship since the early 1980’s. He also claims to be celibate.
