FIFA 23s Team of the Week 14 puts four players in the top spot

Publish date: 2024-04-19

FIFA 23’s fourteenth Team of the Week (TOTW) has put four players at the squad’s highest rating.

Two of them are double-boosted cards, and all four have a rating of 88.

Inter Milan striker Lautaro Martínez is the first to get the highest-rated card, while Germany’s Jonas Hofmann is the second.

Unusually, this week's two double-boosted cards have also received the highest rating of 88 this week.

This could be because TOTW 14 has lower overall improvements than we’ve seen in other squads.

Montpellier’s Stephy Mavididi is one of two double-boosted cards this week, with a 12-point boost from 76 to 88.

More in FIFA 23

The other is Leipzig’s Dani Olmo who has a smaller boost of 6-points from 82 to 88.

These are the four highest-rated cards in FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) this week, but there are also several other cards in the high 80s.

If you download the FIFA 23 companion web or mobile app, you can open FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) packs on the go.

This means you can start building your team ready to play in the game.

The TOTW cards will show up as special cards in FUT packs this week, with boosted stats.

This week’s team will be available via FUT packs until Wednesday, February 8, 5pm UK time.

After that, these boosted cards will only be purchasable via the transfer market, usually for high prices.

Here is a list of all the players in FIFA 23’s Team of the Week and their boosted stats:

  • 88 - Martínez, Hofmann, Mavdidi, Dani Olmo
  • 87 - De Paul, Berardi
  • 86 - Luis Alberto
  • 85 - Álex Remiro, Ginter, Valencia, 
  • 84 - Dante
  • 83 - Pezzella, Di Gregorio, Frankowski, Svanberg, Orsolini, Pavlidis, Onisiwo
  • 82 - Garcia
  • 81 - Douvikas
  • 79 - Burić, Guarrotxena
  • 77 - Campbell
