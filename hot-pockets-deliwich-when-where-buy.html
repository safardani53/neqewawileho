What is the the Hot Pockets Deliwich?

Publish date: 2024-08-31

HOT Pockets is releasing a new sandwich called the Deliwich.

The new Hot Pockets Deliwich will be a first of its kind for the company as it is the first cold sandwich for Hot Pockets.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When are they hitting shelves?

Hot Pocket's new Deliwich sandwiches will be available to purchase at the end of May 2022, PEOPLE reports.

These new Hot Pockets are a new experience entirely for customers as the sandwiches do not require heating.

Customers can eat the Deliwich sandwiches cold or heat them.

Perfect for lunchtime, the sandwiches are stored in the freezer and put out to thaw for two to four hours to enjoy for lunch.

In a statement obtained by PEOPLE, Hot Pockets brand marketing manager Andy Godish spoke about the new product.

"New Hot Pockets Deliwich is a high-quality sandwich with a little more 'chill' and major convenience for busy parents and their kids," Godish said.

"Deliwich offers an easy, great-tasting lunchbox option that stays fresher longer and is always ready to enjoy by the time lunch rolls around."

What flavors do Hot Pockets Deliwich come in?

Hot Pockets Deliwich sandwiches come in four flavors for everyone to enjoy.

The four flavors include Cheddar & Ham, Turkey & Colby, Cheese Melt, and Pepperoni & Mozzarella.

These sandwiches contain deli meat with cheese wrapped in a soft roll.

For a vegetarian option, Hot Pockets offers the Cheese Melt flavor that does not contain meat.

Hot Pockets posted a story on their Instagram on May 19 to confirm the new Deliwich sandwiches are coming "soon."

How much do the Hot Pockets Deliwich sandwiches cost?

Hot Pockets Deliwich sandwiches will be sold in a box of four sandwiches.

Each box will be priced at around $7.49, according to a press release.

The price for each box will vary based on the store location.

Customers can use the official store locator on the website to find their nearest Hot Pocket retailer.
