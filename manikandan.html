Manikandan  BodyBuilding Records / Fitness Fanatic

He was overweight in middle school and began working hard to slim down. He joined a gym in 2015 and began working on his body. After that, he began competing in various bodybuilding competitions. He won the title of ‘Mr. World 2017’ and a gold medal after a lot of hard work. China hosted the competition.

Manikandan was crowned Mr. Tamil Nadu in 2017 and Mr. South India in 2017 in the same year. He was the first Indian to place in the top five in the international men’s physique competition “FIT PARADE 17.” He also won the ‘Mr. According to his official Instagram bio, world 2021’ competition in Men’s Physique.

Manikandan is also a businessman who has opened his gym. Toneez Fitness Centre is the name of his gym. His gym has locations in both Chennai and Hyderabad. He uses his social media account to promote his gym and provides gym hopefuls training.
