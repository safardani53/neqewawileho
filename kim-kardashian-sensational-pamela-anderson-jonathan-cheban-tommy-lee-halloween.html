Kim Kardashian looks sensational as she dresses up as Pamela Anderson with best pal Jonathan Cheban

Publish date: 2024-04-08

KIM Kardashian looked sensational as she dressed up as Pamela Anderson with best pal Jonathan Cheban posing as Tommy Lee.

The superstar was channelling the blonde bombshell's look from the 1999 MTV Video Music Awards with her rocker ex hubby.

Kim, 38, was a dead ringer for Pamela in her iconic 90s look, and her ever-loyal sidekick, Jonathan, could have easily been mistaken for Motley Crue drummer Tommy Lee.

Wearing a white corset, sequined trousers, a blonde wig and a fluffy pink hat, Kim looked sensational in the eccentric ensemble.

And Jonathan seemed to take his role seriously - clad in the same oversized mac, converse trainers and beaded dreadlocks as Tommy.

The pair were certainly a sight to behold as they attended the party of Travis Scott - the boyfriend of Kim's little sister Kylie Jenner.

The mum-of-three had taken to her Instastories to show the sexy look to her 120million followers - posing and strutting in her wardrobe.

Kim had feigned outrage at fellow party-goers not recognising the outfit and blasted them for being "too young to know."

Will Smith's son Jaden was one who didn't get the reference, however, sister Kylie immediately clocked the iconic look - immediately running over to Kim screaming "Pam Anderson."

Kim's really been getting into the festive spirit - having earlier donned Victoria's Secret Angel wings with her sisters.
