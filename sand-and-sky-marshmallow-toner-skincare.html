This product acts as an exfoliant and cleanser, as it contains AHA, BHA, as well as Niacinamide, hyaluronic acid and marshmallow root extract.

AHA and BHA works to exfoliate and cleanse the skin, while niacinamide works to unclog pores, reduce the appearance of enlarged pores, and reduce the impact of environmental aggressors to achieve a clearer and blemish free complexion.

Hyaluronic acid is a popular skincare ingredient, which works to nourish the skin, and rehydrate the dermis, for plump, glowing skin.

Marshmallow Root Extract is essential to calm and soothe skin, as it has anti-inflammatory properties.
