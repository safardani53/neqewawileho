This Morning's Phillip Schofield sets crisp packet on FIRE after microwaving it in bizarre Snapchat

Publish date: 2024-05-03

PHILLIP Schofield appears to have a new hobby as the This Morning presenter spent his Sunday night shrinking crisp packets and even set one on FIRE in the microwave.

The telly favourite has been on a break from the ITV morning show and spent his week off in Dubai where bad weather put a dampener on his holiday.

However, it seems he knows how to make his own fun and taking to Snapchat, Phillip posted a picture of a charred crisp packet and wrote: "When the conversation gets round to how we used to shrink crisp packets in the oven...but you forget at what temperature" followed by a laughing emoji.

He then shared a number of pictures of shrunken Hula Hoops packets as he got the hang of making miniature size crisp packets.

However, it all went wrong when he tried to do it in the microwave.

He shared a video of a red packet quickly exploding within seconds of him turning it on before the video cut off.

Phillip wrote: "Btw...don't do it in the microwave! Thankfully my finger shot off record so you didn't get the language".

Fans were loving his antics with one follower writing on Twitter: "Watching Phillip Schofield microwaving a crisp packet on snapchat might have been the only highlight of my evening."

Another said: "Highlight of today, Phillip schofield puting a packet of crisps in the microwave."

A third wrote: "Omg Phillip Schofield putting a crisp packet in the microwave not realising it would set on fire😂😂😂".
