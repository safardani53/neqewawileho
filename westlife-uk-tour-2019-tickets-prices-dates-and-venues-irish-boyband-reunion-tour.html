Westlife UK tour dates 2019 - Tickets, prices and venues for the Irish boyband's reunion tour

Publish date: 2024-06-08

WESTLIFE, with almost 60million record sales and a fan base desperate for more, are back!

Six years after they last left the stage, Westlife has returned and will discuss their reunion on tonight's episode of the Jonathan Ross Show- here is all you need to know about their 220th-anniversary tour...

When are tickets on sale for the Westlife tour?

The band, made up of Shane Filan, Kian Egan, Mark Feehily and Nicky Byrne, will take to the road again in 2019.

Tickets are on sale NOW and you can buy them at AXS.

They're also on sale at Live Nation, Ticketmaster and Seetickets - prices vary venue to venue.

It's being billed as The Twenty Tour, celebrating 14 No1 songs over 20 years of hits.

More on Music

Will Westlife be releasing new music?

The boys are working with former manager Louis Walsh again alongside Simon Cowell’s former right-hand man Sonny Takhar on new music.

They also have Ed Sheeran to thank, as he has penned their comeback single.

The band have admitted superstar Ed was a major factor in their decision to finally return after years of rumours.
