Who is Lucy Hale? Pretty Little Liars star who suffered a nude photo leak heres what we know

Publish date: 2024-09-12

THE 27-year-old star of Pretty Little Liars was the victim of a phone hacking.

Nude photos of Lucy Hale were shared by hackers after her iCloud was broken into.

Who is Lucy Hale?

She is an American actress and singer.

Hale first came to prominence as one of the five winners of the reality show American Juniors - a spin off of America Idol.

She is best know for her role as Aria Montgomery in Pretty Little Liars.

The role won her a People's Choice Award for Favourite Cable Actress in 2014.

In 2014 she also released a debut studio album, Road Between.

She has appeared in How I Met Your Mother, The O.C. and Baby Daddy.

What happened after her iCloud was hacked?

Nude images of the star were leaked on Wednesday and she has threatened the site which published the images with legal action.

The defiant star told those responsible to “kiss [her] a**” in an unapologetic statement which she released on social media.

The note read: “Well, I wasn’t going to comment on something so ridiculous, but since 2017 is gonna be all about speaking my truth… I’m going to say something.

She continued: “Once again, a woman in the public eye was violated, stolen from and her private life and body were exposed for anyone to see.

What is Pretty Little Liars?

It is an American teen drama, mystery-thriller television series loosely based on the popular book series of the same title.

It first aired in 2010 on ABC Family.

It has been confirmed that the series will end with Season 7 in 2017.
