Real Housewives of Dubai: Who is Lesa Milan Hall?

Publish date: 2024-09-03

BRAVO'S new reality show, The Real Housewives of Dubai, first premiered on June 1, 2022.

Among the stars is Lesa Milan Hall, who lives in Dubai with her family.

Who is Lesa Milan Hall?

Lesa Milan Hall is known for her career as an entrepreneur.

She is the founder of My Little Makers, a children's educational brand that creates early development toys, books, and media.

The reality star also owns the luxury maternity brand, Mina Roe.

According to her website, she has worked with several celebrities, including Beyoncé, Serena Williams, Katy Perry, Gigi Hadid, and Khloe Kardashian.

When she is not at work, she can typically be found at home with her husband, Richard Hall, and their three children: Maximilian, Sebastian, and Kristian.

What is Lesa Milan Hall's net worth?

Over the years, Lesa has helped launch two successful brands, which has led to her extensive net worth.

While an exact number is unclear, it is estimated to be between $5million and $9million.

This estimate comes from the success of her businesses, among other ventures.

How did Lesa meet her husband?

Before the premiere of The Real Housewives of Dubai, Lesa sat down with Distractify and revealed how she met her husband.

"He was on vacation in Miami, and I was going through a horrible breakup," she told the media outlet.

"My girlfriends dragged me out to Miami, on a girls' night, and we met."

Lesa added: "We were long distance for about a year, and then, eventually, he popped the question, and I moved to Dubai."

Before meeting her husband, Lesa studied journalism and minored in fashion at Florida Atlantic University in Boca Raton.

"I like to say I was made in Kingston, packaged in Miami, and then exported to Dubai," Lesa continued.

Richard is known for his career as a real estate developer and financier.

How can I watch The Real Housewives of Dubai?

The Real Housewives of Dubai marks Bravo's 11th city in the franchise and follows the lives of six women who run business empires.

The show can be watched live on Bravo each Wednesday at 9pm ET or streamed through Hulu.
