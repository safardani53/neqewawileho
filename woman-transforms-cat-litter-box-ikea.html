A WOMAN has upgraded her cat's litter box - and you can't even tell it's in the room anymore.

Annika Hinds, a proud thrift and DIY queen, shared how you can too.

Taking to social media, she showed off her cat's exposed litter box which sat next to her desk.

"For the longest time, I've been wanting to upgrade this litter box to one that doesn't look like a litter box and it's more discreet while being functional for my cat," she said.

She then explained how she bought a $50 storage bench from Ikea.

However, when she built it, she left one side exposed that would be the entrance to the litter box.

"The good thing about this piece is that you can decide which side you want to leave open and also depending on your space and where you want to put it," she explained.

"It also has a removable top that makes it really easy to access the litter."

Once she had built, she realized that her cat litter box was slightly too large for the space so she proceeded to cut off the edges.

To protect her cat from hurting themselves when they jumped in, she bought a rubber window sill protector.

It had an adhesive on the back that she could easily stick to the litter box to seal the edges.

"It turned out perfect, it fits in with my decor, and you would never know it's a litter box," she concluded in her video.

Many people applauded the DIY-er in the comment section and one person even suggested covering the inside with waterproof padding to protect the furniture piece itself.
