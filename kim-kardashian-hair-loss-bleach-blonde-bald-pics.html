Kim Kardashian’s Hair Loss — Is She Going Bald After Bleaching Blonde?

On April 7, we saw that Kim is sporting new long extensions, but could it be to add thickness to her thinning hair?

Kim raised plenty of eyebrows and almost broke the internet (again) when she debuted her platinum blonde hair during Paris Fashion Week. Even though many reports claim that she was wearing a wig, she was totally rocking the real deal and she was in the salon adjusting her blonde hair color nearly every week!

Even though it seems as if Kim is innocently changing her hair yet again by adding extensions, reports say that she’s actually hiding her hair loss! A source told InTouch magazine that, “She’s losing her hair because of all of the stress… She even has a bald spot!”

Kim Kardashian & Kanye West’s Marriage — On The Rocks & Causing Kim To Lose Hair?

While Kim’s hair did definitely cause a stir during fashion week, it may have also created some drama in her marriage!

InTouch also reports that it was Kanye who pushed for Kim to bleach her hair blonde, and that afterwards she “was crying and enraged and accused him of destroying her looks.” It allegedly led to their “worst fight ever,” and only added to the stress that Kim is experiencing over wanting to have a second baby and dealing with Kanye’s crazy work schedule.

There’s no doubt that Kim’s hair took a beating and may be damaged from going platinum blonde so drastically, but do you think she’s really losing her hair? The photo that InTouch used (Kim with dark hair, above) is actually from 2012.
