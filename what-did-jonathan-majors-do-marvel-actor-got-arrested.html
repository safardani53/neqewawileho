What Did Jonathan Majors Do? Marvel Actor Got Arrested

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Jonathan Majors was detained on domestic abuse allegations on March 25. The victim was a 30-year-old female who stated she was attacked, according to the Deputy Commissioner of Public Information. They also stated:

“Officers apprehended the 33-year-old male without incident.” The deceased suffered slight head and neck injuries and was taken to a nearby hospital in a safe condition.”

Jonathan Majors was in a fight with his fiancée.

According to reports, Majors and his partner fought on their way home from a club in Brooklyn. Majors and his partner were texting when another lady confronted him about the same. Majors was allegedly irritated by her actions, and he finally seized her hand and struck her. The capture was the outcome of a 911 contact with the authorities around 11:14 a.m. Other accusations such as choking, attack, and intimidation are being investigated.

Carrie Gordon, Jonathan’s agent, also spoke to a few news sources, saying that he is harmless and that they are working hard to resolve the situation as quickly as possible. Meanwhile, an NYPD spokesman told The Associated Press that Majors is no longer in detention. Jonathan Majors’ latest endeavor, Magazine Dreams, will premiere on December 8th of this year. Searchlight Pictures confirmed the information, and the film has already garnered favorable feedback at this year’s Sundance Film Festival.

Elijah Bynum wrote and produced the film, which is about Killian Maddox, played by Majors, who attempts to become a well-known figure in the world of bodybuilding despite many obstacles, including coping with feelings of isolation and rage problems, as well as caring for his grandpa.

Jonathan Majors is well-known for his portrayal of Kang the Conqueror.

Jonathan is well-known for his role as Kang the Conqueror in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The character first appeared in the Disney+ series Loki, which debuted in 2021, and he is expected to return for the second season, which is set to release this year. Meanwhile, he played Kang the Conqueror again in Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania, which was published on February 17, 2023. Despite receiving conflicting reviews from reviewers and fans, the picture earned approximately $465 million at the box office.

Majors portrayed Damian “Diamond Dame” Anderson in Creed III, the third entry in the Creed franchise and a sports drama film. The film, produced by Michael B. Jordan, has grossed nearly $228 million at the box office.
