[PHOTOS] Julia Louis-Dreyfus Emmys Hair &amp; Makeup Tousled Curls Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Julia Louis-Dreyfus relied on hairstylist Matthew Monzon, who used StriVectin HAIR products. He told us about the inspiration: “Upon seeing the gorgeous Carolina Herrera gown, as a collective, we decided the hair needed to be somewhat easy, playful, and with a bit of whimsy. We went with Hollywood glamour, but loosened up to make it feel a bit more modern.”

He started with fresh, clean hair by using StriVectin HAIR Color Care Shampoo & Conditioner.

After washing and conditioning, he used StriVectin HAIR Max Volume Root Lifting Spray on the roots and then used StriVectin HAIR Ultimate Restore Densifying Foaming Treatment from mid-length to the ends using a wide-toothed comb.

He blowdried hair with his fingers, lifting at the roots for volume. He used a round brush on the ends to create some polish.

[jwplatform hnJEfyRJ]

He used a one-inch curling iron around the hair, varying the sizes of the sections of hair. He wrapped hair around the iron in alternating directions, which creates a more tousled, modern effect that looks more natural.

Finally, he used about a quarter size amount of StriVectin HAIR Color Care Vibrancy Booster from mid-shaft to ends for a vibrant, shiny finish.

Her makeup was really natural and pretty, with smokey eyes and a nude lip.
