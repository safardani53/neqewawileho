Meet Singles Inferno season 3 cast Kim Gyu Ri, Lee Gwan Hee and more on Instagram Tue 12

Publish date: 2024-05-10

The Single’s Inferno season 3 cast are officially here, hoping to find love on the Netflix series. Following the success of seasons 1 and 2, the hottest and most flirty singles search for love on a deserted island. The only way to escape? To match for a romantic night in ‘paradise’.

Single’s Inferno season 3 cast

  • Kim Gyu Ri
  • Lee Gwan Hee
  • Choi Hye-Seon
  • Choi Min-Woo
  • Lee Jin-Seok
  • An Min-Young
  • Yu Si-Eun
  • Son Won-Ik
  • Yun Ha-Bin
  • Yun Ha-Jeong
  • Park Min-Kyu

Kim Gyu Ri

Age: 28

Job: Fashion model

Instagram: @citruszl

Kim Gyu-Ri, not to be confused with the popular South Korean actress, revealed on the dating show that the path of modelling opened up for her when she was in college and she “just kept going for it.” 

“I just found I really like standing in front of the camera and being myself,” Gyu-Ri added. She is currently working in the fashion industry, and spends most days doing beauty shoots.

Lee Gwan Hee

Age: 35

Job: Basketball player

Instagram: @leegwanhee0429

Singles Inferno season 3 star Lee Gwan Hee is a basketball player who is confident he’ll have the ladies making the first move – and that’s mainly just by saying his name due to being “popular”.

The cast member reckons producers should “thank” him for making season 3 the best season of the popular show yet. Netflix‘s Kwan Hee is in for a busy ride and is clearly feeling “confident”.

Choi Hye-Seon

Age: 24

Job: Bioinformatics major at Ewha Woman’s University

Instagram: @hazelchoiii

Choi Hye-Seon is often found in the gym and expressed how confident she feels about her appearance. She is looking for a “masculine man” to match the five weight-training work-outs she does a week.

Spoilers: She was matched with athlete Lee Gwan Hee for her first date! She told him she studies natural sciences, was at the prestigious Ehwa Women’s University, and has done internships in her career path.

Hye-Seon currently studies at Durham University after earing her bachelor’s degree at EWU.

Choi Min-Woo

Job: Model

Instagram: @choimin_woo

Choi Min-Woo on Single’s Inferno’s season 3 cast likes older women. He likes females who seem “innocent, elegant, bright and cheerful” and hope they are attracted to his “height and shoulders”.

He plans to do his best to win his ideal girl over if he meets her on Single’s Inferno…

Lee Jin-Seok

Age: 36

Job: Bakery café owner

Instagram: @seok_lj

Lee Hin-Seok’s wide chest is “what defines his build” and gives him the most confidence. He does weight training every day, and is confident of his physical strength, while people tell him he looks intimidating

They also tell him he looks “cute when he smiles because it catches them off-guard.”

An Min-Young

Age: 26

Job: Pilates studio director and instructor

Instagram: @my_floria_

An Min-Young describes herself as “bright and bubbly” while most people think she’s lovely. She’s confident that she can use her charm and joined in hopes of finding a guy who can make her heart race.

The Singles’ Inferno cast member for season 3 was matched with Lee Jin-Seok.

Yu Si-Eun

Age: 26

Job: TV personality and influencer

Instagram: @yoo__si

Yu Si-Eun has been popular since she was young – both in school, and after she started working. People tell her that she’s lovely, and she thinks it’s because she’s expressive and has a cute personality.

She was the 2022 Miss Korea beauty pageant runner-up!

Son Won-Ik

Instagram: @hiwonik

Often told he has a “boyish charm,” Son Won-Ik is “optimistic and honest”. He likes to try new experiences and is adamant he will escape the inferno by finding a lady who matches him.

Yun Ha-Bin

Job: Actor

Yun Ha-Bin is often told that his eyes make him appear good-natured and “attractive”. Once he sets a goal for himself, he will not ever give up as he likes to shake things up.

Yun Ha-Jeong

Age: 25

Job: Model

Instagram: @hi__jjeong2

Yun Ha-Jeong confesses that there’s “always one or two guys that fall in love with me”. But Yun Ha-Jeong seemed jealous as Lee Gwan-Hee revealed he is attracted to other women.

She left Yu Si-Eun feeling jealous as Ha-Jeong vied for the attention of Park Min-Kyu. But she doubts there will be any other female on the show who has a prettier smile than her.

Park Min-Kyu

View Instagram Post

Job: Police officer

Instagram: @ssrt_mk

Park Min-Kyu chose to go with Ha-Jeong, leaving Si-Eun disheartened. The two spent quality time in Paradise before the same twist led to Ha-Jeong meeting Gwan-Hee.

He is the competitive type who will go straight for the person he likes once he finds her. The cast member reckons his height and broad shoulders are attractive and has many nicknames because of his shoulders.
