Daughter of EastEnders star Gillian Taylforth probed by police following 1am bar brawl

Publish date: 2024-03-07

THE daughter of EastEnders star Gillian Taylforth was at the centre of a 1am bar brawl yesterday.

Police were called after a shouting match between Jessica Taylforth, 25, and three friends and a group at another table in the beer garden turned violent.

The scrap, in which customers and door staff were punched and a barmaid had clumps of her hair ripped out, was captured on CCTV cameras at The Swan in Hoddesdon, Herts.

One customer told The Sun on Sunday: “Jessica was walking around the pub like she owned the place.

"She turned to somebody and said, ‘What are you looking at’, then it all kicked off.”

Another said: “It just exploded out of nowhere. Men, women and door staff all got punched and kicked.

“A member of staff had her hair pulled out as she tried to protect a young lady who was being punched in the face.

“The poor girl had her glasses ripped off her face and thrown on the floor.”

She said Jessica, actress Gillian’s daughter by late partner Geoff Knights, played innocent when police arrived.

She said: “Jess calmly walked away with her boyfriend as if nothing had happened.

“It’s disgusting that someone can act so calm after such a shocking scene. One of the girls who was hit was in floods of tears. About five people were hurt and all want to press charges.”

Police said they were called to reports of “an altercation involving a number of parties.”

They appealed for information and said a probe was under way.

Jessica, of Biggleswade, Herts, works for Thomson Holidays, according to her Facebook page.

In 2007, she was left fighting for life after suffering head injuries in a car smash. Her mum, 61, returned to Albert Square as Kathy Beale in 2015.
