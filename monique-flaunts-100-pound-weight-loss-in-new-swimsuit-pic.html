Mo'Nique Flaunts 100 POUND Weight Loss in New Swimsuit Pic

Publish date: 2024-09-24

If you’ve been wondering what ever happened to Mo’Nique, we’ve got an update for you: She’s working her butt off.  

And her waist and her thighs and her arms.

The actress recently shared a swimsuit pic to Instagram, showcasing a staggering 100 pound weight loss – and she looks incredible.

She posted the pic above, posing with two friends in a black one-piece with a cinched waist that does all the talking for her.

"HEY SISTERS. THE 40 AND OLDER CLUB. AT THE BEACH HAVING A BALL," she captioned the photo.

How’d she do it?

Duh, she’s a celebrity and has means and resources.

Still, her determination is something money can’t buy.

Amazing job. Keep it up, girl!
