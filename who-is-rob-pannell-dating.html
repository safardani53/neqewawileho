Rob Pannell is an American professional lacrosse player who plays for the New York Lizards and formerly for Cornell University. He is the second leading scorer in men’s Division I history, and holds the Cornell and Ivy League all-time scoring records. He was the Ivy League’s first ever three-time Player of the Year award winner. He was voted the NCAA’s Outstanding Player of the Year in Men’s Lacrosse in both 2011 and 2013 when he was awarded the Lt. Raymond Enners Award by the USILA.
