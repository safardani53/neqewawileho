Rihanna releases 120 visual autobiography including 1,050 photos of herself The Sun

Publish date: 2024-03-28

POP icon Rihanna has released a book of 1,050 pictures of herself - fetching almost £90,000.

The “visual autobiography”  which includes a version with its own viewing stand as well as a handle, includes images of her relaxing at the beach and posing on steps.

Rapper Cardi B, 27, paid £87,000 for a rare edition at an auction, which came complete with a 2,000 pound marble pedestal.

But if you haven’t got that cash to hand, a £120 version is out this week - small change by comparison.

Rihanna, 31, real name Robyn Fenty said: “I am so excited to share this collection of incredible images.”

At the book’s American launch at the renowned Guggenheim Museum last week Rihanna said: “They say a picture is worth a thousand words, and I believe that,”.

“I tried to pick the ones that I looked cute in first,” she joked.

She added: “I worked so hard and so much, consistently when I was young. Now its even crazier. I’m like wow, there was a day I only did music?”

Cardi B - real name Belcalis Marlenis Almanzar, bought the Supreme edition entitled Stoner, which has already sold out.

Another version, costing £140, comes with a steel green and chrome tabletop book stand.
