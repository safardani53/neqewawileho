Loretta Lynn Owns a Whole Town in Tennessee!

Publish date: 2024-03-24

Country singer Loretta Lynn, whose astounding 58th studio album, Full Circle, is out now, shared 25 fun facts about herself with Us Weekly.

1. My first job was picking strawberries.

2. I own a whole town with its own post office: Hurricane Mills, Tennessee.

3. I’ve seen a ghost there.

4. I love peanut butter anything.

5. Bob Dylan is my favorite songwriter.

6. I cook a pot of beans and a skillet of homemade cornbread once a week.

7. I don’t like to hear myself sing.

8. My favorite movie is Tombstone with Kurt Russell.

9. I have nine cats.

10. I collect salt and pepper shakers.

11. I still grow a garden every year.

12. I have never worn red nail polish.

13. I like to be alone. That’s when I write.

14. I’ve never liked going to awards shows or parties.

15. I love Pink. She sings with heart.

16. I read the Bible almost every single night.

17. My bad habits are candy and snack foods.

18. I had a replica of my childhood home in Kentucky built on my ranch.

19. I just had pink carpet put in my house. My kids are still making fun of me.

20. I have six children, 21 grandchildren and 16 great-grandkids.

21. I can’t swim.

22. I picked Sissy Spacek to play me in Coal Miner’s Daughter.

23. I’ve always wanted to go to Australia and China.

24. My favorite perfume is Joy by Jean Patou.
