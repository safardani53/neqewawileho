Who is Michael McIntyre's wife Kitty and do they have kids?

Publish date: 2024-03-18

MICHAEL McIntyre has become a familiar face on TV and the comedy circuit over the years.

The comedian often uses his family life as a source of hilarious material for his stand-up shows, which includes his beloved wife Kitty Ward.

Who is Michael McIntyre's wife Kitty?

Kitty Ward is the wife of world-famous comedian Michael McIntyre.

She is no stranger to fame though, as she is the daughter of the late film star Simon Ward and sister of Young Sherlock Holmes actress Sophie Ward.

But unlike her celebrity relatives, Kitty keeps her private life under wraps.

It is thought that she works as an aromatherapist in London.

When did they get married?

Michael and his wife Kitty tied the knot in 2003, but the comedian first met his bride when he first met her.

He wrote in his 2010 book Life and Laughing: "She was the girl I had been looking for. In the romantic comedy that was my life, this would have made a good ending.

But the comedian managed to woo Kitty, and the pair began dating.

Do they have any children?

The pair have two children together, Lucas and Oscar.

Both of Michael's sons have been the subject of his comedy sketches.

In a sketch about about the nightly struggle of putting his sons to bed, the comedian joked: “You never love your children more than when they’re unconscious, but still breathing.”
