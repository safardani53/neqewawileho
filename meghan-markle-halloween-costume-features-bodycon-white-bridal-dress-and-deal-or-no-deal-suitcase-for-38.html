Meghan Markle Halloween costume features bodycon white 'bridal' dress and Deal Or No Deal suitcase f

Publish date: 2024-04-08

WE WERE obsessed with Meghan Markle's wedding look last year, and now you can copy it - for Halloween, anyway.

Fashion brand Yandy is selling an "American princess" costume, clearly inspired by the Duchess of Sussex, which is now in the sale for just under £20.

  • American Princess costume, £19.94 from Yandy - buy now

The main part of the outfit consists of an off-the-shoulder white dress.

It has quite a few similarities to the Givenchy gown Meghan, 38, married Prince Harry in on May 19 2018, including cropped sleeves.

But it's more than a little bit shorter and tighter - and we're pretty sure the Queen wouldn't have approved if Meghan had rocked up to Windsor Castle wearing it.

The costume comes complete with a tulle veil and a bejewelled tiara.

And on Yandy's website, the model is even sporting Meghan's trademark messy bun.

She's also carrying a blazer over her shoulder - a potential nod to her Suits days - and there's a Deal Or No Deal suitcase by her side, though these aren't included.

Meghan was a suitcase girl on the US version of the game show back in 2006.

The costume's description reads: "You're living an actual fairy tale!

"Get ready to walk down the royal aisle in this exclusive American Princess costume."

Fancy "living an actual fairy tale" on October 31? At $24.97 (£19.94) in the sale, it's pretty much a bargain.

You'll need to add on $9.95 (£7.55) for shipping from America and wait seven to 15 days for it to arrive.
