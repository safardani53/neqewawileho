It’s officially the holiday season! Julianne and Derek Hough host ‘The Wonderful World of Disney: Magical Holiday Celebration,’ which will air on November 26. See the best photos.

It’s time to celebrate the holidays at the happiest place on Earth! Sibling duo Julianne Hough and Derek Hough team up to host The Wonderful World of Disney: Magical Holiday Celebration, which airs November 26 at 8 p.m. on ABC. Julianne and Derek will be performing a holiday medley.

Additional performers include Mickey Guyton, Michael Bolton, Tori Kelly, Andra Day, Chrissy Metz, Robin Thicke, The Smashing Pumpkins, American Idol winner Iam Tongi, and more. DJ Khaled will be making a special appearance as well.
