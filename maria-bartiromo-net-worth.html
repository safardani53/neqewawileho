What is Maria Bartiromo's net worth?

Publish date: 2024-03-03

FOX Business Network's Maria Bartiromo has a long list of successes that led her to where she is today.

Bartiromo has been with Fox since 2014 and most notably hosts Mornings with Maria and Sunday Morning Futures on Fox News channel.

Who is Maria Bartiromo?

Bartiromo was born in Brooklyn, New York.

She has two older siblings. Bartiromo's father owned a pizza shop.

Bartiromo attended Long Island University before transferring and graduating from NYU. She studied journalism and economics.

In 2010, she returned to her alma mater where she taught as an adjunct professor for three years.

More on entertainment

Bartiromo was first hired as an intern at CNN in 1988.

During this time, Bartiromo built a portfolio for herself and sent her clips to the president of CNBC who hired her almost immediately.

She moved to FOX Business in 2014.

Bartiromo was the first journalist to report from the floor of the New York Stock Exchange.

What is Maria Bartiromo's net worth?

Celebrity News Worth reported that Maria Bartiromo, 54, makes about $10million per year.

To date, Maria Bartiromo's net worth is an estimated $50million.

In addition to Mornings with Maria and Sunday Morning features, Bartiromo is the author of seven books.

Is Maria Bartiromo married?

Bartiromo has been married to her husband Jonathan Steinberg since 1999.

According to Heavy, Steinberg is the son of billionaire Wall Street executive Saul Steinberg.

Steinberg attended the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania but dropped out to work for Bear Sterns and start his own publishing company.

Bartiromo and Steinberg do not have any children.
