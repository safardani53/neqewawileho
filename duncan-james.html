Who is Duncan James:

Duncan James is a famous Pop Singer. He was born on April 7, 1978 and his birthplace is Salisbury, England. Duncan is also well known as, English actor and singer formerly of the popular UK boy band Blue. He went on to star as Ryan Knight in the E4 soap opera Hollyoaks.

Duncan is originated from England. He previously dated former Blues singer Tara Palmer Tomkinson.
