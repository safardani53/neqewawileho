Aaron Hernandez Tweet After Murder Of Odin Lloyd — Creepy

Less than 24 hours after Aaron allegedly murdered Lloyd, he tweeted, “good morning” to all of his fans.

In all, the message has been retweeted a whopping 2,622 times so far.

Odin Lloyd Allegedly Murdered — Details Behind His Death

The DA said Odin was shot with a .45 caliber semi automatic weapon before his body was discovered near Aaron’s North Attleborough, Mass. home on June 17, according to TMZ.

Prosecutors reportedly said that Odin and Aaron were in contact just 10 hours before his death. Furthermore, keys were allegedly found in Odin’s pocket to a car that had been rented by Aaron.

Prosecutors believe Odin recently said something to Aaron that destroyed his trust, giving Aaron a motive to kill. (Could they have gotten this information from Aaron’s girlfriend?)

Aaron Hernandez: More Info About The Patriots Tight End

Aaron was on the New England Patriots from 2010 to 2012, and was dropped from the team immediately following the arrest.

The Patriots made waves in 2012 when they signed him to a five-year extension, which included a $12.5 million signing bonus. It was the second-largest extension ever in the NFL, and it showed the total faith the team had in his abilities.

Aaron and Odin grew close as Odin dated Shanea Jenkins, the sister of Aaron’s fiancée, Shayanna Jenkins.

Aaron and Shayanna have an 8-month-old child together.

The New England Patriots React To The Charges

HollywoodLife.com received a statement from the team in the wake of Aaron’s arrest:

A young man was murdered last week and we extend our sympathies to the family and friends who mourn his loss. Words cannot express the disappointment we feel knowing that one of our players was arrested as a result of this investigation. We realize that law enforcement investigations into this matter are ongoing. We support their efforts and respect the process. At this time, we believe this transaction is simply the right thing to do.
