Demi Burnett Was Diagnosed With Autism After Undergoing A Psychological Evaluation

Publish date: 2024-04-10

The 26-year-old actress, who starred in Colton Underwood’s season of The Bachelor in 2019, announced the news on her Instagram page, along with a series of slides detailing the illness and how to help someone with autism. She also included a “trigger warning” in the post, just in case. Burnett desired a higher quality of life and told her followers that she would reveal more of her tale and how she “came to all this point” in the future. The graphics in the thread detailed the autism spectrum, how to help a neurodivergent individual, what ableism is, a chart of autistic tendencies versus trauma symptoms, and much more.

Many members of the Bachelor Nation, including Onyeka Ehie, Kirpa Sudick, and Maurissa Gunn, expressed their support for Burnett after her diagnosis. Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental impairment that may create social, communication, and behavioral issues in which the individual may interact, behave, or learn differently than others, according to the CDC.
