Christie's serene professional career of making cars run smooth, hit the brakes in 2016, after a close encounter with a killer.

But the doctors assured Christie the cancer was in initial stages and could be removed via a one-day surgery. And Christie went through the surgery along with the cost of her voice as the doctor had removed part of her trachea because cancer had spread.

Christie’s voice box was cut, and she had to wait a good eight months for her voice to come back. After a series of radiation treatments and consuming the correct medicines for a whole year, Christie was announced cancer free.
