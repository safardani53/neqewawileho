Tricky quiz challenges drivers to recognise 15 road signs but NO ONE has got them all right

Publish date: 2024-05-30

IF you passed your driving test years ago and the theory exam feels like a distant memory, you might want to test yourself with this quiz.

Drivers are being challenged to name the 15 road signs, and while some are fairly standard there are a few fakes to try and throw people off.

Car dealership Robins & Day devised the quiz, after research revealed a third of drivers wouldn't be able to pass their test if they took it today.

That seems to be the case with the quiz too, as the dealership confirmed no one had scored full marks yet.

The top score - and the one to beat - is just 61 per cent, so can you do any better?
