Black Snow Is Set To Released On Sundance Now Soon

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Black Snow, Sundance Now’s latest original series, is set to launch on Thursday, February 23, 2023. The story revolves around the tragic murder of a youngster in the 1990s. The investigation lies cold for many years until a fresh investigator takes up the case in 2020. The program stars Travis Fimmel in the primary role, with many others playing important supporting parts. Lucas Taylor, a well-known director, is in charge of the series.

Black Snow at Sundance Now: Trailer, Plot, What to Expect, and More Details

Sundance Now released the official trailer for Black Winter on January 26, 2023, and it provides a glimpse into the several significant events that will take place in the new series. The trailer begins with an interesting voiceover that perfectly establishes the tone and highlights the conceptual components of the series:

“The most difficult aspect of cold cases is determining who individuals were back then.”

The idea of the program is immediately established in the trailer, as the protagonist sets out to delve further into the startlingly strange death of a young girl called Isabel. He takes up the case more than 20 years after she died and attempts to uncover the truth. According to AMC Networks, below is the official summary of the show:

“Half coming-of-age story, part mystery, Black Snow recounts the investigation of a small-town murder that shook the Australian South Sea Islander community. Isabel Baker, a seventeen-year-old girl, was killed in 1995.”

The following is the rest of the description:

“The crime stunned her close-knit neighborhood and saddened the area. The crime was never solved, and the murderer was never apprehended. The uncovering of a time capsule in 2020 reveals a truth that leads cold-case investigator James Cormack (Fimmel) on the trail of the murderer.”

Viewers should anticipate an atmospheric crime thriller that examines some of the most terrible realities about human nature based on the trailer and description. The series will start with two episodes on February 23, 2023, and fresh episodes will be released every Thursday afterward.

Additional information about the Black Snow cast

Travis Fimmel plays Jimmy Cormack in the criminal drama series. Cormack is a talented investigator who takes on a case that has been dormant for many decades. Cormack urgently seeks to probe deep into the 1995 death of a young girl in 2020. The plot is presented from his point of view, and his character defines the show’s emotional center.

In the teaser for the series, Fimmel looks gorgeous, conveying the numerous complicated shades of his character with great ease and painting him with a hint of mystery that adds to his allure. Travis Fimmel has been in a number of famous and highly acclaimed films and television programs throughout the years, including Vikings, Raised by Wolves, and Delia’s Gone, to mention a few.

Actors such as Jemmason Power, Gulliver McGrath, Jimi Bani, and Erik Thomson appear with Travis Fimmel in crucial supporting parts. Eleven Eleven, Texas Rising: The Lost Soldier, Harrow, and many more films have been created by Lucas Taylor. On Thursday, February 23, 2023, the first two episodes of Black Snow will be available on Sundance Now.
